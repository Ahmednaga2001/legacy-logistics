All goods insured. Professional certified drivers with 10+ years experience. Euro 6 fleet with GPS tracking for safety and efficiency. 24/7 service and strong logistics team to provide excellent solutions for your transport needs.

By Road:

Whether you have a single shipment, a few pallets, or multiple full loads every week, we can handle your cargo and provide you with the best solution for your road freight. With our own feet and traffic hubs located all over Europe, we make sure your cargo is delivered safely and efficiently.

We offer road transport services for:

  • * Full load shipments
  • * Part load shipments
  • * Groupage shipments
  • * ADR shipments
  • * Time-sensitive shipments
  • * Domestic shipments can be completed within 24-48 hours. For more remote destinations in Europe, we offer delivery within one to five days at all destinations.

By Air:

Sometimes cargoes need to reach their final destination with top speed. To ensure the reliable transportation of your cargo whenever you need it we have designed our Air Cargo Solutions to suit your needs. As with all our solutions, working with Legacy for your air freight means you’ll be working with a dedicated partner who is committed to ensuring the most reliable journey for your cargo from start to finish.

By Ocean:

Whatever you are importing or exporting, our ocean shipping solutions get all products to their destination. From selecting the right container for your goods to loading safely and efficiently at the portside, to monitoring the condition of your container throughout its time at sea, we will support you at every step. Working with Legacy will plug you into a global network of experts. We’re committed to offering competitive rates and fast transit times along with personalized support. Inland shipping entails transporting cargo using a country’s transport infrastructure. This could be by road, rail, river, and other networks. Legacy's solutions are designed to provide you with a one-stop shop for your inland shipping requirements. Just leave it to us to move your cargo

By Rail:

Whatever your cargo, you can count on us to provide the most suitable intermodal solution to cater to your needs. We can offer you intermodal services serving a minimum of five of the main ports in Europe: Bremerhaven, Hamburg, Rotterdam, Antwerp, and Trieste. Our intermodal offerings are designed to complement our global network and provide efficient connections to all parts of the world.

Warehousing through the EU:

Your needs do not start and end when your cargo leaves the site. Our extensive storage and warehousing services ensure the smooth transition of your cargo through every phase. Understanding that the ability to store cargo both at the port and beyond, is crucial. We continue to invest in flexible and innovative warehousing and storage solutions. Through our global network we can offer a door-to-door logistics service – enabling you to move cargo to and from any worldwide destination.
